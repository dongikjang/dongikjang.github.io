Statistician spatio-temporal data analysis and visualization with R

Research interests

  1. Multiscale methods in statistics: Wavelets, empirical mode decomposition with Hilbert-Huang transform, directional wavelets, and synchrosqueezed wavelet transform.

  2. Function estimation: Wavelet shrinkage, smoothing splines, robust regression, quantile regression, and Kriging.

  3. High-dimensional data analysis: Sparse modeling and estimation, variable selection, and statistical learning.

  4. Time series analysis: Spectral analysis, nonstationary processes, and statistics in finance.

  5. Functional data analysis: Functional dynamic model, functional factor model, and functional clustering.

  6. Spatial statistics: Spatio-temporal analysis.

  7. Statistical computing: Parallel computing, distributed computing with Hadoop, statistical visualization, and optimization.

  8. Applications: Statistical climatology and bioinformatics.
